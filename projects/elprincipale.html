"El Principale" - Online University Certificates

  • Tech Stack: Angular, Spring Framework : Spring boot, Spring MVC, Spring Security, Lombok, MySQL, I Text Pdf

A Spring/Angular platform which allows users automatically generate and deliver certificates to students.

Users can log in using credentials provided by the university's administration. They can then apply to obtain a certificate and await approval from the administration. Once approved, they can download the automatically generated certificate.

The school administration's responsibilities include adding students to the database and reviewing pending certificate requests.

A small test plan was developed to assess various aspects of the application, employing tools such as Selenium, JMeter, and Postman.
